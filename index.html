How to Fish on Survivor

On Survivor, you not given that much food, the only kinds of food that you're going to get on Survivor are reward foods that you must win, rice by coming to a deal with Jeff Probst at a merge immunity challenge,the fruit on nearby palm trees from your camp, and the fish in the ocean.

So your tribe decides that they want fish for a breakfast, lunch, or dinner. You go out into the ocean to try to catch the fish. But how do you catch them is the problem. Well, this webpage will show you how to catch the fish by your tribe camp properly so that way, your tribe will have a nice fishy dinner that night.

How to Spearfish with a Hawaiian Sling

To use a Hawaiian sling, the first thing you want to do is grab the elastic rubber tube and pull it down towards the spearhead. When you let go, the spear shoots or jumps forward because of the impactful rubber tube contracting.
How to Build a Fish Trap
  1. Attach the short bamboo sticks to the larger hoop with string.
  2. Attach the smaller hoop to the ends of the bamboo sticks to form a funnel.
  3. Attach the long bamboo sticks to the top hoop of the funnel with string.
  4. Attach a second hoop to the mid-section of the bamboo sticks.
  5. Attach a third hoop to form a larger funnel.
  6. Close off the funnel, by securely tying the ends of the bamboo sticks together.
  7. And there you have it, a nice and secured fish trap capable of catching any small fish in the sea.
How to Use a Cast Net
  1. Attach the hand line loop around your wrist and make small coils in your hands.
  2. Pull the horn up through the draw strings to the end of the hand line.
  3. Grip the net one third down with the same hand.
  4. Check and make sure that lead line is untangled.
  5. Hold the lead line in both hands.
  6. Swing your hands from right to left and let go of the net.
  7. The net should open and trap fish underneath it.
How to Use a Seine Net

There are two sides of a seine net, one with floats and the other with weights. Make sure the net isn't tangled when setting it up to ensure that the fish don't escape. To use it, have two people stand on opposite ends and walk into the water until the net is taught and then walk back to the beach. Make sure the net is in an arc shape form so that way the fish will swim into it.
